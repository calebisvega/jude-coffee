Our Story

In 2022, Jude was founded by Jordan and Kelsey Willcox in sunny Lakeland, Florida. From its inception, Jude has been designed to be a mobile third place for people to gather.

Since then, David & Caleb have inherited this treasure of a coffee company. In the past years, we've
been serving coffee under a similar sensibility with Dumb Coffee, our
last venture in hospitality.

Whether you're a roaster with loads of experience or this is your first time trying to order a drink with a long Italian based name, we're here for you.

We'll try our best to have a dialogue that leads to you experiencing the depth and kindness of others. We may not know all the correct answers, and that's okay, (everybody knows capppucccino is incredibly difficult
to spell.)

We believe that both genuine care and others-centered hospitality are a large part of what makes us better humans.

In an age where deep ties are fading and neighbors have grown farther estranged, our little pocket of Central Florida needs a reason to hang out and get to know each other. In fear of this sounding like a grand manifesto, we'd like to simply introduce Jude this way.
Jude for all.
