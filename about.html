In 2022, Jude was founded by Jordan and Kelsey Willcox in sunny Lakeland, Florida. From its inception, Jude has been designed to be a third place for community to gather and to embody a spirit of familial celebration by serving beautiful coffees with a heart of intentioned hospitality.

Since then, David & Caleb have inherited this treasure of a coffee company. In the past years, we've been serving Coffee under a similar sensibility with Dumb Coffee, our last venture in hospitality.

About us

Whether you're a roaster, a coffee producer with loads of experience or this is your first time trying to order a drink with a long Italian based name, we're here for you. We'll try our best to have a dialogue that leads to you experiencing the beauty and kindness of others. We may not know all the correct answers, and that's okay, (everybody knows capppucccino is incredibly difficult to spell.)

We believe that both genuine care and others-centered hospitality are a large part of what makes us better humans. We believe that in an age where deep ties are fading and neighbors have grown farther estranged now more than ever our little pocket of Central Florida needs a reason to hang out and get to know each other. In fear of this sounding like a grand manifesto, we'd like to simply introduce Jude this way.

Jude for all.

More than just coffee

We love coffee. Not just for the taste, but for the moments it creates — the pause before a ceremony, the warmth between conversations, the quiet energy of a morning meeting done right.

We're Caleb and David, two friends in Lakeland who saw a gap between the coffee people drink every day and the coffee served at the events they care about most. Jude was born to close that gap.

To us, coffee at an event is a connection — between host and guest, between craft and occasion. When you book Jude, you're part of something we care deeply about: doing one thing well, and doing it for people who notice.

The cart

Our mobile setup travels across Central Florida — weddings, corporate events, markets, and private gatherings. We arrive early, build the bar, dial in the espresso, and serve until the last cup.

Sourcing

We source beans from Valor Coffee, one of the Southeast's finest third-wave roasters. Combined with house-made syrups and precise extraction on our La Marzocco GS3, every drink reflects the same standard we'd hold in a brick-and-mortar café.
