About

Built on craft, driven by community.

Jude started with a simple belief: the coffee at your event should be as intentional as everything else.

Our story

We're Caleb and David — two friends in Lakeland who saw a gap between the coffee people drink every day and the coffee served at the events they care about most. Jude was born to close that gap.

We built a mobile cart around equipment we'd trust in any specialty café: a La Marzocco GS3 espresso machine, an Anfim Caimano grinder, and beans sourced from Valor Coffee — one of the Southeast's finest third-wave roasters. No shortcuts. No compromise.

Whether it's a wedding, a corporate gathering, or a Saturday market, we show up early, dial in our shots, and serve every cup like it matters. Because at your event, it does.
